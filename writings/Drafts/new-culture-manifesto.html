A New Culture Manifesto

Let us say that Old Cultures are the cultures we have inherited and New Cultures are the cultures which we are consciously creating for ourselves, our communities and all of humanity. Cultures consist of cultural content and are structured by contextual paradigms which need to be distinguished before we can engage with them consciously.

Cultures exist along many dimensions, including

  • Economic
  • Psychological/Spiritual
  • Social/Political
  • Physical/Scientific/Technological
  • Philosophical/Mathematical

Acting as individuals we have little power to shape and impact culture, as we are unable to engage with culture along enough dimensions. Cultures are best enaged with by groups and communities with high levels of

  • Awareness
  • Diversity
  • Partnership
