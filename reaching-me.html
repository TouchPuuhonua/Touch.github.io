Getting Ahold of Touch

Sometimes I'm hard to get ahold of — really! When I'm intently focused on a project or when I'm close to overwhelm I literally don't notice my phone alerts, I have no sense of the passage of time, what day it is, etc. And despite this I really want to hear from you and right away if we had an agreement to be in communication. What follows are some tips for getting or being in communication with me. Let me know if these are not sufficient and/or if you have suggestions for me and/or need to communicate your frustration about this matter!

Urgent or time-sensitive short messages

  • Text — especially if it's really short and simple
  • Phone Call — especially if I haven't replied to your urgent text
    • repeat until you get ahold of me!

Non-urgent short messages

  • Start by texting me
  • If it's regular, e.g. multiple times a week, shift to WhatsApp

Long messages

  • Send as EMail or WhatsApp (medium)
  • If this is not part of an ongoing conversation:
    • Text me that you just sent me a long message via medium

Even Better?

  • I prefer Signal texts to SMS texts if you're on Signal
  • I like videoconferencing with Zoom
  • If you're friends with Head-Touch on FB
    • Messages go to my phone
    • Great for important or time-sensitive messages
