Serious Questions

  • What quality and quantity of life is in your future?
  • Will you have abundant love in your life?
  • Will you and those you love
    • Experience increased quality of life as your lives go on?
    • Live as long as you wish or have your lives cut short?
    • Learn to express yourselves fully, including your love for one another and your general joy of living?
  • Will you let go of and get over those things which diminish the quality of your life?
    • Fears, resentments, regrets?
  • Will you experience accomplishment throughout your life??
    • Will you experience an ongoing sense of making a difference in the quality of your own life, in the lives of those you love, in your community, and in the world?
    • Will your capacity to achieve and your gusto for participation in ever greater projects increase naturally over the years?
    • Will you embrace new areas of expression, develop new skills, explore new areas of participation over the years?
    • Will all of your capacities expand? Will you become
      • more loving and lovable?
      • more intelligent and creative?
      • more knowledgeable?
      • more wise?
      • more joyous and light-hearted?
