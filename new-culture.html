Touch on New Culture

There is no official definition of New Culture and in fact there can't be: New Culture is being co-created in communities all over the world. It is an exploration of what is possible in expressing fundamental values and it is either alive and a work-in-progress or it is not New Culture! This webpage presents some of my thoughts on New Culture and why it inspires me..

Some terminology I like to use about culture

Default Culture
I use the term Default Culture to refer to the common culture we see on TV and on the street. There are good and bad aspects of Default Culture. Default Culture is a simplified lowest-common-denominator culture. It is handy as a place where people from differing cultural backgrounds can meet and everyone can know the rules. I first heard the term Default Culture used in this way in the Burning Man Community and I liked it for it's descriptive and non-judgemental character.
Old Culture
I use the term Old Culture to refer to the culture we inherit from our family and community of origin. Old Culture may be similar to or quite different from Default Culture depending on our individual circumstances. As with Default Culture, we are likely ambivalent about it and we probably hope for something better for the future. I distinguish Old Culture to contrast with how I distinguish New Culture,
New Culture
I use the term New Culture to refer to the culture we are engaged in creating, in solidarity with other creative people in our community and in solidarity with the New Culture explorations going on world-wide.. New Culture is not some future Utopia but an ongoing creation which we test out by living it right now. It is always a work-in-progress which we are co-creating. Rather than trying to change the whole world according to a conceptual ideal, New Culture aims to discover better ways to live and organize community locally and then allow those discoveries to diffuse outwards into the world. As New Culture spreads and is adapted and remixed, we get to receive as well as give away updated versions of New Culture and allow the world to be transfomed gradually from many sources. If you think this vision is unrealistic then I ask you: Where do you think Old Culture came from? I first encountered the term New Culture used in this way when I attended the extraordinary Network for New Culture Summer Camp retreats. Every since I have been looking at every group and humanistic organization with the question How are they contributing to the creation of New Culture?. And I'm asking the same thing of myself!

Some Avenues to New Culture

Organizations Empowering New Culture

I have had the privilege of participating with a number of organizations which offer programs which nourish and empower New Culture. I have obtained enormous personal value from participating with the organisations and that value has impacted the quality of my whole community.

The Burning Man Global Community

Burning Man began as a festival in San Francisco and is now a network of local communities spanning the globe. In addition to the huge annual Festival which has moved to Navada's Black Rock Desert there are scores of other large gatherings. In my region of San Diego County we hold one or two large gatherings a year of more than 1000 people, but more importantly we have several local community events going on every week of the year.
