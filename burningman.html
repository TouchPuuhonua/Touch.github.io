Touch on Burning Man

Burning Man began as a festival in San Francisco and is now a network of local communities spanning the globe. In addition to the huge annual Festival which has moved to Navada's Black Rock Desert there are scores of other large gatherings and of greatest importance: unlimited opportunities to participate in Burning Man culture every day.

In my region of San Diego County we hold one or two large gatherings a year of more than 1000 people. Local Burning Man participants create multiple local community events every week of the year. Nearly all events are open to anyone interested in participating. Perhaps of even greater importance, people in the Burning Man culture have an expanded level of participantion in the general political and cultural life of our region. I expect that this is true of all local Burning Man participants and communities worldwide.
